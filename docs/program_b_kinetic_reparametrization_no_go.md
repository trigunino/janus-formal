## Result

For a positive plus-sector kinetic coefficient and a negative minus-sector
coefficient, every surjective change of the two mode variables preserves both
a positive and a negative direction. Therefore an invertible field
redefinition, diagonalization or mode rotation cannot repair the published
relative kinetic sign.

## Remaining physical atom

A viable completion must still construct one genuine escape mechanism:

- remove the negative mode by a derived constraint;
- make the second metric auxiliary;
- construct a positive physical PT/Krein completion;
- or use positive Einstein–Hilbert coefficients and move the signed-mass
  information into matter charges/interactions.

## Failure criterion

Any proposal that only applies an invertible change of field variables while
leaving both spin-2 modes propagating fails this gate.
