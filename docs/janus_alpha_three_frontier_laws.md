# Janus Alpha Three Frontier Laws

Purpose: isolate the three hardest remaining non-rustine routes for deriving
`alpha / E_global / L`.

Baseline:

- `alpha` is currently a global state-sector label;
- observations may select it, but that is not no-fit;
- a no-fit route must derive a selector from internal physics.

## 1. Unimodular / Four-Form Sector

Idea:

`E_global` is a conserved four-form flux or unimodular integration constant.

Interpretation:

The universe belongs to a flux sector. `alpha` is global, like an ADM-like
charge, but potentially quantized.

Already investigated:

- CandidateMechanismMatrix has a four-form gate;
- the `alpha <-> E_global` map is available.

Missing:

- Janus-derived four-form action;
- charge or membrane unit;
- PT boundary law selecting one flux sector;
- proof that the selected sector gives the published `alpha` scale.

Status:

promising but not closed.

## 2. Weyl / Conformal Anomaly State Law

Idea:

The non-throat PT/Weyl branch is classically scale-free, but quantum anomaly
terms break scale invariance and generate `L`.

Interpretation:

The global scale is not topological. It is selected by quantum fields living on
the Janus PT/Weyl background.

Already investigated:

- non-throat Weyl-cusp branch closed the conformal geometry;
- the missing data are exactly `L`, clock, source, and boundary state law.

Missing:

- Janus field content;
- anomaly coefficients;
- renormalization prescription tied to PT;
- anomaly-induced stress mapped to `E_global` or `L`.

Status:

best new route adjacent to the current Weyl-cusp branch, but not closed.

## 3. Euclidean `S4_L/RP4_L` Saddle

Idea:

`L` is selected by a finite Euclidean projective saddle, via
`dS_E(L)/dL = 0`.

Interpretation:

The universe is selected by a projective no-boundary-like state, not by late
observations.

Already investigated:

- `S4_L -> RP4_L` is the faithful projective topology;
- global-action route exists as an open alpha selector route.

Missing:

- finite Euclidean bimetric Janus action;
- boundary/corner/crosscap prescription;
- path-integral contour or measure;
- matter/vacuum state on the saddle;
- Lorentzian continuation to the published Janus branch.

Status:

clean but most radical. Not closed.

## Current Verdict

All three routes are credible research frontiers. None currently derives a
no-fit `alpha`. The strongest practical order is:

1. unimodular/four-form sector;
2. Weyl/conformal anomaly law;
3. Euclidean `S4_L/RP4_L` saddle.
